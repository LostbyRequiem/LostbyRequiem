About

LostbyRequiem is an emerging lo-fi shoegaze and ambient indie rock artist from Edmonds, WA, crafting deeply introspective music built on memory, atmosphere, and emotional honesty.

With a focus on storytelling through mood and subtle detail, LostbyRequiem explores themes of grief, longing, and transformation. Each track is intentionally imperfect, layered with ambient noise, tape hiss, and delicate vocal delivery.

More than just songs, LostbyRequiem’s music feels like pages from a journal: quiet, raw, and deeply human. It invites listeners into a space of reflection, where silence and sound coexist.
